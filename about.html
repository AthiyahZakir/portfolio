Fashion & Embroidery Art

Fashion is my way of expressing confidence and creativity — storytelling through fabric and color. I enjoy the detail-oriented art of tambour embroidery, where every thread contributes to something beautiful and intricate.

I believe in thoughtful design — choosing quality, creativity, and sustainability over fast trends. From outfits to embroidery, design is where I find balance between discipline and imagination.

Art meets elegance — fashion and embroidery as forms of storytelling.
